HabitSpark AI Documentation

Everything you need to know about HabitSpark AI — from your first spark to advanced AI coaching, analytics, and social features.

🚀 Quick Start Guide

Get up and running in under a minute.

Installation

  1. Download HabitSpark AI from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android).
  2. Open the app and create an account using your email, or sign in with Facebook for seamless social integration.
  3. Grant notification permissions so we can send you daily reminders.

Onboarding

On your first launch, a 5-step onboarding flow walks you through:

  • Selecting your goals (Get Healthier, Be More Productive, Find Inner Peace, Level Up My Life)
  • Choosing up to 3 habit categories from 12 options
  • Picking your coaching tone (Gentle, Motivational, or Neutral)
  • Setting your daily reminder time

Starter habits are created automatically based on your selections — you're ready to spark!

Dashboard Overview

🏠 Home Screen

  • Momentum Ring — a 0–100 score showing recent activity, weighted toward the last few days
  • Trend Badge — ↑ Rising, → Steady, or ↓ Dipping compared to last week
  • "Your Rhythm" chart — bar chart showing completion rates by day of the week
  • AI Coach card — personalised insights with contextual badges (AT RISK, NEW PB!, TRENDING UP)
  • Habit cards — tap to complete, with weekly dot trackers, streak counts, and smart labels
  • Quick Stats — 14-day rate, best streak, and this week's sparks

🧭 Navigation

  • Home — daily dashboard with habits and AI insights
  • Coach — AI coaching cards, habit suggestions, per-habit tips
  • Stats — full analytics with charts, heatmaps, and performance bars
  • Badges — achievement system with progress rings
  • Leaderboard — friend rankings and social features (when enabled)

✨ Adding & Managing Habits

Build your personalised habit collection.

Adding a New Habit

  1. Tap the ✨ Explore Sparks icon in the top bar.
  2. Browse 12 categories — each with a gradient icon and description.
  3. Tap a category to add it. Free users watch a short ad to unlock; Spark Pro users get instant access.

Editing & Deleting

  • Tap a habit card to edit details like name or category
  • Long-press a habit card to delete it
  • Duplicate habits are automatically prevented — you can't add the same habit twice

Categories

Habits are organised into 12 categories, each with a unique colour and emoji: Healthy Eating 🍎, Fitness 💪, Saving Money 🐷, Mental Wellness 🧘, Productivity ⚡, Sleep Hygiene 🌙, Learning 📚, Relationships 💝, Sustainability 🌿, Career Growth 💼, Housekeeping 🏠, and Journaling ✏️.

🤖 AI Coach & Suggestions

Personalised coaching powered by local analytics + GPT-4o-mini.

How It Works

The AI Coach operates in two layers:

Layer 1: Local Analytics (Free)

An on-device analytics engine analyses your completion dates, streaks, weekly patterns, and day-of-week trends. This powers the AI Coach card on the home screen with instant insights — no API call needed, no subscription required. A built-in database of 1,231 coaching tips across 82 categories provides actionable advice for every habit, served instantly from your device.

Layer 2: GPT Coaching (Spark Pro)

A compact summary of your analytics is sent to GPT-4o-mini for deeply personalised coaching. The AI references your specific habits, streaks, and patterns — like "You're strongest on Tuesdays at 92%!" rather than generic advice.

What You Get

  • Hero coaching cards — prioritised by urgency: at-risk habits → declining trends → personal bests → celebrations
  • Habit suggestions — AI recommends new habits based on gaps in your routine (e.g., "You track fitness but not nutrition")
  • Per-habit tips — tap "Tips →" on any habit for 3 actionable coaching tips
  • Adaptive tone — all messages adapt to your chosen tone (Gentle, Motivational, or Neutral)
💡 Free users get local AI insights and coaching tips from a built-in database of 1,231 tips — no internet needed. Upgrade to Spark Pro for GPT-powered personalised coaching, suggestions, and per-habit tips without ads.

👥 Social & Sharing

Compete with friends and stay motivated together.

Connecting with Friends

  1. Enable social features in Settings → Social.
  2. Go to Add Friends on the Leaderboard tab.
  3. Search by username or share invite links via WhatsApp, SMS, email, or any app.

Leaderboard

  • Podium — top 3 friends displayed with 🥇🥈🥉 medals
  • Rankings — everyone ranked by total completions, with rank change arrows
  • Expand cards — tap any friend to see their full stats and earned badges
  • Social actions — send 💪 Challenges and 🎉 Celebrations
🔒 Social features are completely optional. When disabled in Settings, the Leaderboard tab is hidden entirely. HabitSpark works perfectly as a solo experience.

🔔 Notifications & Reminders

Stay on track with customisable nudges.

Daily Reminders

Set during onboarding or anytime in Settings. Choose from preset times (Morning 8:00 AM, Afternoon 1:00 PM, Evening 8:00 PM) or set a custom time.

Encouragement Settings

Fine-tune your motivational messages in Settings → Encouragement:

  • Toggle psychological encouragement on/off
  • Choose tone: Gentle, Balanced, or Motivational
  • Enable/disable specific message types: Milestone Celebrations, Struggle Support, Consistency Praise

🔧 Troubleshooting & FAQ

Common issues and how to fix them.

📱 App not loading

Check your internet connection and restart the app. If the issue persists, clear the app cache in your device settings.

🔕 Notifications not appearing

Ensure notification permissions are granted in your device settings. On Android, check that battery optimisation is turned off for HabitSpark AI.

🤖 AI tips not refreshing

Free users need to watch an ad for fresh tips. Spark Pro users get instant access. If tips are stuck, try clearing the app cache in Settings.

🌐 Language not switching

After changing the language in Settings, restart the app for the change to take effect. HabitSpark supports 7 languages.
